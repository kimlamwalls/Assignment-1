While I think it’s important to take test results like these with a grain of salt as they are only an estimation of one's personality I think they can still have some useful relevance at least initiating some self reflection. CONTINUE….
As expected I am a little further towards the introverted and intuitive spectrum. This reinforces that I need to focus on regular communication and bouncing ideas off others around me to get the most out of a given team task.
This also is likely to have the follow on effect of alleviating any negative self critique and keeping me on task.

While forming a team I hope to discuss these goals of open communication to encourage both others and myself to keep each other honest, focused and keep everyone involved feeling supported.
